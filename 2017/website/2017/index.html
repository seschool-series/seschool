Welcome!

The 2017 Summer School on Software Engineering hosted by the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano is an event (September 4th-7th, 2017) featuring four tutorials delivered by leading researchers in the Software Engineering field.

The third edition of the school is organised by the Software and Systems Engineering Research Group at unibz and is mainly intended for PhD and Master students interested in the latest findings in Software Engineering.

Partecipants will enjoy the beauty of the nearby Dolomites from the town and during the SeSchool excursion.
