Welcome!

The 2018 Summer School on Software Engineering hosted at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, Italy on July 9-12, 2018 is an event  featuring tutorials delivered by leading researchers in the Software Engineering field.

The fourth edition of the school is organised by the Software and Systems Engineering Research Group at unibz and is mainly intended for PhD and second-year Master students and practitioners.

This-year theme is Software Engineering for Mobile Applications.

Participants will also enjoy the beauty of the nearby Dolomites from the town and during the SeSchool excursion.
